About Cayman Chromatic Series 3

“For the third series of Underwater Photography art we chose to diverge from the bright and bold colours of the previous two collections and create something more minimal and elegant. These more dark and mysterious images of Cayman sea-life offer a more sophisticated style of decor.

Whilst experimenting the photo editing we found that our recent photos of squids, jellyfish and stingrays stood out beautifully against a dark background. We really toned down on the colours and instead used contrast to let the subjects stand out against black or white background.
