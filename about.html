About Us.

About Us.

Underwater photography is fascinating, and the Cayman Islands is the perfect place to practice the art. Cayman Chromatic is a collection of underwater photography, featuring anything from dynamic fish life to tranquil coral heads, transformed into striking visuals.

We are Tom and Luxi, and scuba diving is our biggest hobby - we go whenever we get the chance. For the past couple of years we've been regularly capturing images in the Cayman seas, and these unusual images lend themselves really well to being turned into bright and vibrant works of art.

Cayman Chromatic is the amalgamation of marine life and modern art, a unique format to show the beauty of the Cayman Islands. We hope you enjoy the collection as much as we do.
