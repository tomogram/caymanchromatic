About Cayman Chromatic Series 1

"The first collection of Cayman Chromatic artworks comes in a variety of bold and vibrant colours and textures, and features sealife such as squids, juvenile spotted drum fish and flamingo tongue sea slugs. This first collection of artworks was a result of a passion for capturing the wonderful variety of lifeforms that are relatively easy to find around the shores of the Cayman Islands.

We also wanted to create something that was also a bit different, and could also work as a decorative art piece. The pop-art style colours with images of the Cayman Sealife make a perfect gift for anyone who loves the Cayman Islands, the sea and the amazing life that it hosts."
