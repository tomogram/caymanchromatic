About Cayman Chromatic Series 2

"After completeing our first collection of artworks we had a better idea of what kind of photographs worked best for maniuplating into colourful, vibrant art works. We had also spent a lot more time scuba diving, exporling the reefs surrounding Grand Cayman and finding all sorts of beautiful and interesting lifeforms like Seahorses, Anemones, Pistol Snapping Shrimp and one of Cayman's most chershed sea creatures - the Hawksbill Turtle.

Our artistic direction also developed, and we went for a more pastel toned, colour palette. We used more colours like pinks, oranges and purples and in many cases tried to create cleaner looking images with less mess to clutter to distract from the subject of the image."
